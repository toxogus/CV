### Smart Factory Capstone Design
**GPSFramework: Global CLIP–PatchCore–SAM for Zero-Shot Anomaly Detection**

A three-stage, zero-shot framework facilitating immediate deployment on novel industrial products by integrating vision-language models with classical anomaly detection and prompt-based segmentation, without requiring category-specific training.

- **Stage 1 — GlobalCLIP**: Utilizes an ensemble of six autoencoders alongside a dual-backbone CLIP architecture, generating coarse anomaly scores through Adaptive Region Selection and Adaptive Anomaly Weighting.
- **Stage 2 — PatchCore**: Employs a WideResNet-50 feature extractor combined with a coreset-reduced memory bank to achieve efficient, patch-level anomaly scoring via nearest-neighbor analysis.
- **Stage 3 — SAM**: The Segment Anything Model acts as a final refinement layer, utilizing prompt-guided inputs to produce precise, pixel-level localization of detected defects.

**Benchmark**: Evaluated on the MVTec AD dataset, achieving competitive image-level AUROC and pixel-level PRO scores in a fully zero-shot setting.
